The Abu Dhabi Chamber of Commerce and Industry

The Abu Dhabi Chamber stands as a testament to the visions of the late Sheikh Zayed, embodying his legacy and aspirations for a prosperous future. Since its establishment in 1969, the Chamber has been able to position itself as the voice of the private sector, playing a crucial role in drawing policies and strategies that have promoted the business environment, not mentioning its instrumental role in supporting the growth of the private sector and bolstering its competitiveness globally. Today, the Chamber continues in its journey to solidify its role as a key player in the national economic landscape by enabling businesses to realize sustainable growth while remaining steadfast in its commitment to fostering excellence and innovation and shaping the future of Abu Dhabi’s economy.
